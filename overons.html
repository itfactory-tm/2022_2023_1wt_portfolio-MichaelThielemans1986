Over ons

In 2001, Cupcakes bakery opened its first location on a quiet street corner in the heart of New York City's West Village. From its inception, Cupcakes bakery has been cherished for its classic American baked goods, vintage decor and warm, inviting atmosphere. In 2007, Cupcakes bakery original owner passed her oven mitts to Steve and Tyra Abrams. Together, the Abrams' have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its first West Village shop to locations worldwide. Today, Cupcake bakery can be found in New York City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Honolulu, Mexico City, Seoul, Tokyo, Moscow City, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Beirut, Kuwait City and Doha.
